Endovascular

CO2 Injector by Angiodroid

This Bologna based start-up has developed the first and only automatic CO2 injector authorised for use during peripheral angiography procedures. Automatic CO2 angiography is a safer, cheaper, more effective and less risky alternative than the use of contrast media containing iodine, a nephrotoxic agent.

We feel inspired by Angiodroid’s revolutionary approach and look forward to bringing this technology to hospitals and patients in Switzerland.
